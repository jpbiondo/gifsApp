GIFS APP

A simple app to load gifs

1. How to use

Go to search page or use top search box and write a topic about the gif, then 8 gifs will should be loaded. There's a sidebar you can toggle and it'll show your ten most recent searches, you can click them to load them again easily.

2. What's next?

This goal of this app is really simple. I built it to get my hands on Angular Framework. In the future I'll refine it more, adding use cases such as:

  1. Top 10 popular gifs
  2. Gifs links
  3. Better card design
  4. More gif info
  5. And more ideas when they come...

Any suggestions or critics are welcome.
